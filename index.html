The idea behind the club

About

There's a word — sonder — for the moment you realise every stranger is living a life as vivid as your own.

Lost Postcard Club is a small archive built around that feeling. A silhouette down a backstreet, a window on a slow afternoon, a place you only passed through — the kind of moment that feels like a postcard someone wrote, sent, misplaced, and that you've somehow found years later.

We call these photographs postcards. Members — really just followers, and people who love film — send them in, and we keep them together in one quiet, growing collection. Mostly 35mm, though digital is just as welcome.

No algorithm to please, nothing to sell you here. Just a place to feel a little nostalgia, a little wanderlust, and a quiet sense of connection to people you'll never meet.

It's found

We add it to the archive and share it on Instagram, credited to you, so others can find it too.

The photograph stays yours. Always.

We never claim ownership of anything you share. By submitting, you're simply giving us permission to show your photograph in this gallery and on our Instagram — and we'll credit you every single time.
